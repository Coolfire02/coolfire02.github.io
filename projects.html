Studio Project 2

Year 1 Sem 2 EOS Project @ NYP

Worked with our Computer Graphics framework built on openGL by our Lecturer, in a team of 5, created a game based on the theme "My City Tour".

We had to apply our knowledge of Data Structures, Game Design, Computer Graphics into the making of our Game.

I focused on creating programming framework creating dynamic systems including: Entity Management, Collision Event & Detection, Buttons and Missions.

2020

Studio Project 1

Year 1 Sem 1 EOS Project @ NYP

This was our first ever Coding Project that we had to work together as a team to create. We decided to make a 2D Fire Fighting game based off the Australia Fires that occured in 2020.

The goal is to do Tasks to earn "Water" to put out fires or earn "Money" to buy better equipment to store or gain more "Water" per minigame.

Since this is 2D, we wanted to make many levels for players to play, as such I made a In-Game built in Level Editor! For us to create unique levels with roads and paths dynamically.
